Volunteer Opportunities

Youth Facilitator

Lead mindset workshops, youth dialogues, and empowerment sessions in schools and communities.

  • 4-6 hours/week
  • Local communities
  • 3-month commitment

Environmental Champion

Lead tree planting campaigns, waste management programs, and community sensitization.

  • 3-5 hours/week
  • Field locations
  • Flexible

Digital Volunteer

Support our social media, content creation, website management, and digital campaigns.

  • 5-8 hours/week
  • Remote
  • 6-month commitment

Media & Communications

Capture stories, photography, videography, and help amplify LEGTA's impact.

  • 4-6 hours/week
  • Hybrid
  • 3-month commitment

Project Assistant

Support program coordination, event planning, and administrative tasks.

  • 6-8 hours/week
  • Office/Remote
  • 6-month commitment

Research & Impact

Help measure our impact, conduct surveys, and analyze data for reporting.

  • 4-5 hours/week
  • Remote
  • 3-month commitment
