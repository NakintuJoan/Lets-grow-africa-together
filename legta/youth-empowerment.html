YOUTH EMPOWERMENT

Transforming minds, discovering purpose, and creating Africa's next generation of leaders

Empowering Africa's Greatest Resource

Young people across Africa face numerous limitations including unemployment, limited opportunities, and inadequate mentorship. At Legta, we believe youth remain the greatest resource for socio-economic transformation when properly aligned in mindset, purpose, and action.

Mindset Transformation

Breaking limiting beliefs and fostering a growth mindset for success

Purpose Discovery

Guiding youth to discover and align with their life's purpose

Leadership Development

Building confident, capable leaders for Africa's transformation

Our Youth Empowerment Programs

Mindset & Purpose Alignment

Transformational workshops that help youth identify and overcome limiting beliefs while discovering their unique life purpose and contribution to society.

  • 3-day intensive workshops
  • Personal vision mapping
  • Strength assessment

Mentorship & Peer Learning

Connecting young people with experienced mentors and facilitating peer-to-peer learning networks for continuous growth and support.

  • 1-on-1 mentorship
  • Peer learning circles
  • Leadership coaching

Young Leaders Fellowship

A 6-month intensive program developing the next generation of African leaders through training, projects, and exposure.

  • Leadership curriculum
  • Community projects
  • Networking opportunities
