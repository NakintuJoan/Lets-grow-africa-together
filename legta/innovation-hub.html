INNOVATION HUB

Where ideas become solutions

Empowering young innovators to solve Africa's challenges through creativity, technology, and entrepreneurship

Fueling Africa's Innovation Economy

We believe that young Africans possess the creativity and ingenuity to solve the continent's most pressing challenges. The Innovation Hub provides the platform, resources, and mentorship to transform ideas into sustainable ventures.

Our Innovation Programs

Featured

Youth Dialogue Forums

Monthly idea-generation sessions where young people collaborate to solve community challenges through innovative thinking.

  • Peer learning
  • Problem-solving
  • Networking

Group Project Incubation

6-month incubation program for youth-led innovative projects. Includes mentorship, seed funding, and workspace.

  • Seed funding up to $5,000
  • Expert mentorship
  • Co-working space

Innovation Challenges

Themed competitions addressing specific challenges in agriculture, health, education, and environment. Winners receive funding and support.

  • Cash prizes
  • Incubation offers
  • Investor access

1
Ideation & Problem Identification

2
Bootcamp & Skills Training

3
Prototyping & Testing

Investment & Scaling

Connect with investors, access joint investment circles, and scale your solution across communities.
