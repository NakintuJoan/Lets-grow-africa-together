Donation FAQs

Yes, Let's Grow Together Africa is a registered non-profit organization. All donations are tax-exempt where applicable. You will receive a receipt for your records.

85% of donations go directly to programs and initiatives. 15% supports operational costs including administration, fundraising, and monitoring & evaluation. We publish annual impact reports with detailed financial breakdowns.

Yes, our donation platform accepts multiple currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, and various African currencies. You can select your preferred currency during checkout.

You can manage your recurring donation through the link in your donation confirmation email, or contact our donor relations team at donations@letsgrowtogetherafrica.org.
